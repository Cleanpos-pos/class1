Online Booking System

4-step booking flow — customers book collection & delivery in under 2 minutes

🛒 Step 1: Select Items

Customers browse your service catalogue with categories, images, and pricing. Add items to basket with quantity controls and notes per item.

🚚 Step 2: Collection Slot

Choose a collection time slot based on your configured availability. Postcode validation ensures you only show slots for areas you serve.

📦 Step 3: Delivery Slot

Select when they want items delivered back. Slots are capacity-limited so you never overcommit. Surcharges applied per postcode area.

💳 Step 4: Review & Pay

Review order, apply discount codes or loyalty points, then pay securely via Stripe Checkout. Automated confirmation email sent instantly.

🏆 Loyalty Points

Customers earn points on every order. Redeem points for discounts at checkout. Configurable earn rate and redemption value per store.

🎁 Discount Codes & Promos

Create voucher codes (% or fixed), BOGO deals, and bundle offers. Set expiry dates and one-time-use limits. Target specific customer segments.

Printing & Labelling

Thermal printing for receipts, garment tags, and bag labels

📄 Customer Receipts

Full receipt with order number, items, prices, totals, and store branding. Printed on 80mm thermal paper via USB or network printer.

🏷️ Bag Tags (Btags)

60mm continuous label with order number, customer name, and barcode. Applied to garment bags for easy identification during processing.

👔 Garment Tags (DStubs)

Individual garment tags in 76mm or 40mm formats. Each tag has order number, item name, and scannable barcode for tracking.

🖨 Print All

One-click "Print All" button generates receipt + bag tags + garment tags in sequence. Saves time during busy intake periods.

Supported Hardware

Bixolon SRP-275III (76mm dot matrix) • 80mm Thermal XPrinter • Brother 60mm continuous label printer • Windows PC (Surface Pro touchscreen supplied)

Payments & Billing
