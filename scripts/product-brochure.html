Schedule & Time Slots

Full control over collection and delivery availability

Define exactly when you collect and deliver. Set time slots per day, manage postcode areas, and control your capacity.

📅 Day-by-Day Control

Toggle each day on or off independently for collection and delivery. Closed Sundays? Just switch it off.

🕐 Custom Time Slots

Create multiple slots per day — "09:00-12:00", "14:00-17:00". Customers choose what works for them.

📍 Postcode Areas

Define your service areas by postcode prefix. Set delivery surcharges and distance limits per area.

🚚 Separate Collection & Delivery

Collection and delivery have independent schedules. Collect Monday-Friday but only deliver Tuesday-Saturday? No problem.

🗼 Postcode-Specific Slots

Different areas can have different time slots. Central postcodes get more options, outer areas get specific days.

🔄 Override Capability

Staff can override delivery dates on any order from the back office — accommodating special requests with ease.
